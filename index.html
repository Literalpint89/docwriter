Connect to Google Docs
Get your access token in 3 clicks:

1. Open OAuth Playground ↗ — it opens pre-configured with the right scopes.

2. Click Authorise APIs → sign in → Exchange authorization code for tokens.

3. Click the button below to paste your token automatically.
Token is valid for 1 hour. The app warns you at 5 min remaining and auto-pauses if it expires mid-session.
